No matter what you do, follow

1 Follow the rules
2 Follow rule number one
3 Your goal is to eat as many dots as possible in order to get a higher score
4 Your school teachers will haunt you with bad grades, so don't let them get near you
5 If a teacher does give you a bad grade, then one of your life points is gone
6 If you reach 0 life points, then GAME OVER
7 Grabbing the special pills will make you crazy, making it possible to bite the teachers which will send them back to their office, thus avoiding the bad grades
8 You can also play with another friend and see who can get a higher score, so feel free to try the multiplayer as well
